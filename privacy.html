Privacy Policy

How we protect your information

Simple Summary: Deck of Chances does not collect, store, or process any personal information from users. We only interact with Instagram to post our daily content.

Overview

Deck of Chances is a lightweight automated application that posts daily short, entertaining predictions to Instagram. The system interacts only with Instagram and Facebook services via their official APIs for the sole purpose of posting content to the associated Instagram account: Deck Of Chances.

What Data We Use

  • The bot uses its own media content generated by the owner.
  • No user-generated data is accessed, requested, or stored.
  • We do not collect any information from viewers or followers of the Instagram account.

Instagram & Facebook Permissions

The application uses the following Meta API permissions to function:

  • instagram_basic - Basic access to Instagram account
  • instagram_content_publish - Permission to post content
  • pages_show_list - View connected Facebook pages
  • pages_read_engagement - Read basic engagement metrics

These permissions are required exclusively to publish the automated daily reel and story on Instagram. No other data is accessed or used.

Personal Information

We want to be crystal clear: Deck of Chances does not collect any personal information. This includes:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • User interactions, comments, or engagement data
  • Device information or IP addresses
  • Cookies or tracking technologies
  • Any other personally identifiable information

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose personal data to third parties, because none is collected. The only third-party service we interact with is Meta (Instagram/Facebook) for the purpose of posting content.

Data Security

Only the application owner has access to the automation system. Access tokens and credentials are stored securely using industry-standard practices and are not shared with any other parties.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You are free to follow or unfollow the Deck Of Chances Instagram account at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
